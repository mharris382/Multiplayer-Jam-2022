## Purpose
Basically a live document outlining everything that is or will be put into the game.

```ad-tip
title: What is Steam Eagles?
collapse: open
For each thing that needs to be developed think about these questions.
- why it is needed 
- what does it contribute to the game
- what needs to be added/changed
- what work is required to include it

```
